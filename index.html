The Snug, 3 Treglyn Farm Cottages


Luxury Self-Catering Holiday Cottage close to Rock, Polzeath and Padstow in North Cornwall

  • The Snug is a cosy, recently renovated grade 2 listed barn conversion. The Snug, 3 Treglyn Farm Cottages is part of a small complex of eight dedicated holiday cottages set in acres of lawns with ample parking, offering a beautiful, quiet and safe location.
  • The beaches of Rock, Daymer Bay and Polzeath are within a few minutes drive, with fantastic opportunities for surfing, walking, cycling, swimming, boating, playing and resting on the beach. With easy access via the Black Tor Ferry to Padstow from Rock, there are many wonderful restaurants for food enthusiasts too. Port Isaac is a short drive away (location of Doc Martin). Cornwall offers many attractions with much to explore.
  • The cottage sleeps 2 in one very comfortable double bedroom. Downstairs is an open plan living space and includes a quality double (4'6") sofa bed. There is a cosy snuggler seat. The new kitchen is equipped to a very high standard, including dishwasher, offering everything needed for a comfortable and restful break.
  • The bathroom is equipped with a shower and heated towel rail.
  • Modern electric heaters throughout providing warmth throughout the seasons including winter.
  • The cottage has access to it's own cottage garden.
  • NO SMOKING/VAPING.
  • One or two, well behaved, small dogs allowed.
